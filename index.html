About Me

Hello! I'm Daniel Jefferson Wellons, but you can simply call me DJ. I'm a 26-year-old recent Computer Science Engineering graduate with a strong focus on Front-End Web Development and Full-Stack Development.

My educational journey began in the summer of 2017, culminating in my graduation in the fall of 2022 from the University. During my time at the university, I achieved not only my bachelor's degree but also proudly represented the University of Toledo Rockets Football Team from 2017 to 2020 as a Place-Kicker, contributing to our victory as the 2017 MAC Champions. In addition to my academic and athletic pursuits, I actively participated in various research teams, which I'll delve into further in the section.

I have decided that I will be using this website moreso as a personal portfolio vs using this as ammo for job hunting now that I have landed my roled at Sponseller Group. I will still continue to pursue web development as it allows me to express my creativity in ways that other fields simply can't. Designing and building interactive websites and applications provides an outlet for artistic expression that I find incredibly fulfilling.

Web development also aligns with my passion for creating dynamic and engaging user experiences. The web is a versatile platform where I can bring my ideas to life, making them accessible to a global audience. It's the perfect medium for my innate drive to make technology more interactive and user-friendly.

Additionally, the ever-evolving nature of web development excites me. The field constantly presents new challenges and opportunities for learning, keeping me engaged and eager to expand my skill set.I'm enthusiastic about the endless possibilities in web development, and I look forward to creating innovative, user-centric experiences that make a difference in the digital world.
